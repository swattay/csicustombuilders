After considerable analysis and deliberation, we have selected the finest Virginia manufacturer of housing components to work with us in the fabrication and assembly of some of the major components of your new home. NationWide Custom Homes shares our passion for quality, value added and state of the art production efficiency. Their individual workers come from a long tradition of craftsmen that are now only found in the rural areas of the Commonwealth of Virginia. They value their jobs and take pride in their work- they are our kind of people.

We are proud to be a NationWide Custom Homes "Authorized Builder". This means that in addition to offering you our Virginia Collection of Component Crafted Homes, and custom designs which are derived from this Collection, we are authorized build you any of the many fine home plans and components offered directly by NationWide.

NationWide's home plans are offered in four general levels of design and finish (Gold, Silver, Village and Cornerstone). While our Virginia Collection of Homes is planned to the higher standards of finish and design appropriate to the sophisticated Washington, D.C. urban market, the NationWide Home plans offer excellent value and design flexibility. We recommend them without reservation and would be proud to build one for your family.
